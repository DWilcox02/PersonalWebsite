Mitiga Internship

September 2021 - Present

Mitiga is an Incident Readiness and Response company based in Tel-Aviv, Israel, with offices in North-East America and London, England. Specialising in cloud and hybrid environments, Mitiga takes a unique approach to incident response where a speedy recovery is number one priority.

My role as an intern includes reports inventorying, forming a library of cybersecurity recommendations, and helping set up Tabletop Exercises within Mitiga's unique incident response platform. I get to practice a wide array of skills, including spreadsheeting, configuration file writing, and proposing features for the Mitiga Incident Response platform. Alongside technical skills, Mitiga has been a fantastic introduction to the world of business. Startup companies are very fast-paced, so I quickly learned organisational and planning skills, as well as strong communication to work with multiple different teams on different projects.
