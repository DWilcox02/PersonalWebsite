Maths Tutoring

January 2021 - Present

Since January this year, I have been tutoring students aged 14-17 in mathematics. The topics we go over include geometry, trigonometry, and algebraic operations. There has been a demonstrated improvement in grades over the past few months, thanks to attentive students and my more down-to-earth approach. I was a student in their position not long ago, so my insight into their technniques, mental stamina, and problem-solving methods has been incredibly beneficial to our one-on-one sessions.

All sessions take place over zoom, using the zoom whiteboard feature. I save and send the notes from every session once we are done so that students may look back on our previous work together. If you are interested, please email me at danielgwilcox@gmail.com.
